Our Vision

Our CEO, Andrei Stroe, founded eNergy GmbH in 2019 to help connect classical music audiences to live performances of the music they love. For millions of people, eNergy is the address for tickets to classical concerts, operas, and ballet performances on stages around the world.

Our Promise

We are an authorized ticketing partner for every performance on our website. eNergy GmbH is proud to cooperate directly with hundreds of arts organizations, so every ticket at eNergy is an official ticket.
